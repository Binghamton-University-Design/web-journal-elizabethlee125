Exquisite Corpse

This project was in collaboration with my classmate, Sarah Teper. We wanted to sculpt a monster by combining different monsters from all around the world. In this piece, you can see Medusa's snakes, the Cyclops' head, a spooky eyeball, the Hulk's chest, an Octopus' tentacle, Pan's Labyrinth arms and hands, and Godzilla's feet.
