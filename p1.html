Mind, Body, and Soul

For me, Mind, Body, and Soul is unique to human existence. Those three components encompass key aspects to our own being. Mind represents our thoughts, intelligence, knowledge and experiences. Body represents the physicality of our existence. Soul represents our energy, drive, and passion. Together, these three key components sets us apart from one another while tieing us all together under the same categories.

This piece focuses on vibrations. The waves radiating out of the body represent our physical and metaphysical energy vibrations. The static line coming from the heart of the body represents our heartbeat. Without our heartbeat, we would not be able to exist on a physical realm.
